Welcome to

Woodbrook Homestay

Woodbrook is a beautiful and serene place located in the heart of Ooty, India. It is a popular destination for travelers who are looking for a peaceful and relaxing vacation. The area is surrounded by lush green forests, tea plantations, and scenic mountains, making it an ideal location for nature lovers.

More About


Our place is located near to Pykara in 15 acres of tea estate, it has 2 full bedrooms, large living area, study area which has been converted as open bed area and 3 balconies with amazing view of estate. We can arrange trekking inside our estate to reach to the top at free of cost.It can accommodate 10 adults & 4 Kids. We have fully function Kitchen and Guest can use Kitchen by self with some minimal charge. Our place has advantage in terms location as it is placed mid of all tourist locations.
